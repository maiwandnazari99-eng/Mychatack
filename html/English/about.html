About Chatack

Fast by name. Faster by purpose.

Chatack, inspired by the Pashto word چتک meaning fast, is built to bring speed, simplicity, and service to every Afghan household. Our name isn’t just a label—it’s a promise : to move quickly, deliver reliably, and connect people with what they need, without delay. Founded by the visionary team at MLA , Chatack is Afghanistan’s first truly integrated digital service platform. From taxi rides to food delivery to online shopping, we bring everyday convenience into one seamless experience—designed for Afghan cities, Afghan users, and Afghan lives.

Chatack was born out of a simple idea: that technology should serve people, not overwhelm them. That’s why our platform is:
Locally Engineered: Every feature is designed with Afghan streets, culture, and users in mind.
Intuitive & Accessible: Easy to use for everyone—from first-time app users to seasoned digital natives.
Culturally Rooted: Our design, language, and service philosophy reflect Afghan values of trust, hospitality, and community.

Chatack began as a local ride-hailing initiative focused on safety, reliability, and ease of access. Today, it’s grown into one of Afghanistan’s most promising service platforms, with:
Hundreds of active drivers navigating all Afghanistan provinces
Delivery partners bringing meals and goods to doorsteps Local merchants expanding their reach through digital storefronts
Together, we’re building a network of opportunity—connecting people, powering livelihoods, and redefining convenience in Afghanistan.

What are our services?

Taxi Services

Chatack’s taxi network operates 24/7 Afghanistan all citys, offering fast, safe, and affordable rides. Whether you're commuting to work, heading to the airport, or navigating city errands, our drivers are trained, verified, and ready to serve. We also offer daily and hourly car rentals with flexible options—Economy, Comfort, and Business—to suit every need and budget.

Food Delivery

Hungry? Chatack partners with dozens of trusted restaurants across Kabul to bring fresh meals straight to your door. From traditional Afghan cuisine to international favorites, our food delivery service is fast, hygienic, and easy to use. Just tap, order, and enjoy.

Online Shopping

Skip the traffic and long lines. With Chatack, you can shop for groceries, essentials, and household items from your favorite local stores —all online. Our delivery partners ensure your order arrives safely and quickly, so you can focus on what matters most.
